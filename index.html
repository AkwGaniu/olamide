The World Is But An Enjoyment

Abdullaah bin Amr bin Aas may Allaah be pleased with reported:The messenger of Allaah (may Allaa's blessings be up on him)said: "This world is but an enjoyment but the best of its enjoymentis righteous woman. every enjoyment in this world is a temporary enjoyment.however,its best enjoyment is a righteous woman who has her eye on hereafter. she is kind of woman describe in this hadeeth of the noble prophet (may the peace and blessings of Allaah be up on him). when her husband looks at her he pleases with her.when he swears an oath concerning her she fulfills it,and when he is away from her,she safegaurd him regard to herself and his wealth.
